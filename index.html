Welcome to My Homepage

I am currently a postdoc research associate at DAMTP, University of Cambridge. I'm a member of the Cambridge Image Analysis group leading by Dr Carola-Bibiane Schönlieb.

I received my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from ENSICAEN and Normandy University, under the supervision of Prof. Jalal Fadili (CNRS, ENSICAEN) and Prof. Gabriel Peyré (CNRS, ENS-Paris). I received my master degree in Applied Mathematics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoqun Zhang. Prior to that I obtained my bachlor degree in Electrical & Information Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecom.
